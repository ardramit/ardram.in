Laboratory Services

The Laboratory Services at Ardram Hospital provide accurate and timely diagnostic support for patient care. Equipped with modern analyzers and handled by experienced laboratory professionals, our lab plays a vital role in early diagnosis, treatment planning, and health monitoring.

  • Blood, urine & stool examinations
  • Biochemistry & hematology tests
  • Microbiology & pathology services
  • Diabetes & lipid profile testing
  • Quick and reliable test reporting
